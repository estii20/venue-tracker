Alpine Wellness Sanctuary

Nestled in the breathtaking landscape of South Tyrol, Adler Lodge Ritten offers an unparalleled alpine wellness experience. This luxury lodge combines traditional mountain architecture with contemporary comfort, creating the perfect setting for transformative yoga retreats.

The lodge's elevated position provides panoramic views of the Dolomites while offering complete tranquility away from urban distractions. With dedicated wellness facilities and authentic mountain hospitality, it's an ideal venue for UK yoga instructors seeking an immersive alpine experience.

Wellness & Retreat Facilities

Dedicated Yoga Studio

Spacious studio with traditional wooden floors, floor-to-ceiling windows, and mountain views. Fully equipped with mats, blocks, and props.

Alpine Spa

Traditional Finnish sauna, steam room, and relaxation areas. Professional massage therapists available for individual treatments.

Nature Spaces

Multiple outdoor terraces and garden areas perfect for sunrise yoga, meditation, and forest bathing sessions.

Organic Cuisine

Farm-to-table dining featuring local, organic ingredients. Vegetarian and vegan options with dietary requirements accommodated.

Luxury Rooms

Comfortable alpine-style rooms with mountain views, private bathrooms, and traditional wooden furnishings for a peaceful retreat experience.

Mountain Activities

Guided hiking trails, nature walks, and seasonal activities like snowshoeing. Perfect for active wellness programs.
