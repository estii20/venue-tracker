Essential Retreat Planning Checklist in Italy

Your complete guide to organising unforgettable wellness retreats in Italy

Planning Guide 15 min read Updated December 2024

Planning a wellness retreat in Italy can feel overwhelming, but with the right checklist, you can create an extraordinary experience for your guests while maintaining your sanity. As someone who has helped dozens of UK wellness professionals navigate the Italian retreat landscape, I've compiled this comprehensive guide to ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

Emergency Preparedness

Medical Emergencies

  • Call **112** for immediate medical emergencies (Italy's unified emergency number).
  • On-site staff trained in basic first aid.
  • Information on nearest medical facilities/pharmacies.
  • Emergency contact numbers for guests' designated contacts.
  • Support for mental well-being during stressful situations.

Fire Safety & Evacuation

  • Clearly marked emergency exits and evacuation routes.
  • Designated outdoor assembly points.
  • Smoke detectors and fire extinguishers on premises.
  • Emergency contact information for fire services.
  • Briefing on safety procedures upon arrival.

General Safety & Security

  • 24/7 on-site staff available for assistance.
  • Secure access points to the property.
  • Emergency communication plan for all guests.
  • Information on local area safety and best practices.
  • Procedures for reporting lost items or suspicious activity.
