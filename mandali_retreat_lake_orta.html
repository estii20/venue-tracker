Mandali Retreat is a unique, non-profit wellness and yoga retreat center nestled high on a hilltop overlooking the stunning Lake Orta, with panoramic views extending to the Swiss Alps. Architecturally inspired by the typical Italian medieval towns, its structures are built using locally reclaimed materials, creating an atmosphere of a quiet, alpine village. Mandali focuses on creating a sacred space for mental, physical, and spiritual well-being, offering a sustainable ecosystem, delicious vegetarian meals, and various retreat programs.
