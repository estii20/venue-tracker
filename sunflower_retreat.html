A Palace Made for Wellness

Nestled in the medieval hilltop town of Casperia, Sunflower Retreats occupies the historic Forani Palace, a beautifully restored Renaissance building that has been thoughtfully converted into a dedicated yoga retreat center.

With over a decade of experience hosting international yoga groups, this venue combines authentic Italian architecture with modern wellness amenities, creating the perfect environment for deep transformation and connection.

Location & Access

Medieval Hilltop Setting

Casperia is a beautifully preserved medieval town perched on a hilltop in the Sabina region of Lazio, just one hour north of Rome. This authentic Italian setting provides the perfect backdrop for a transformative retreat experience.
