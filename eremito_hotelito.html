Planning Your Retreat

Best Times to Visit

Eremito is magical year-round, but each season offers unique benefits:

  • Spring (March-May): Mild weather, blooming gardens, perfect for outdoor meditation
  • Summer (June-August): Warm days, cool stone interiors, ideal for early morning yoga
  • Autumn (September-November): Harvest season, cozy fireplace evenings
  • Winter (December-February): Most contemplative period, intimate candlelit atmosphere

Getting There

Nearest Airport: Rome Fiumicino (FCO) - 2.5 hours by car
Alternative: Perugia Airport - 1.5 hours (smaller airport)
Train Option: Rome to Terni, then taxi (recommended for eco-conscious groups)
Transfer Service: Private transfers can be arranged through Eremito

Dining Experience
