Faloria Mountain Spa Resort is a prestigious 5-star hotel nestled in Cortina d'Ampezzo, the heart of the majestic Dolomites. Renowned for its blend of traditional alpine charm and modern luxury, the resort offers an unparalleled retreat experience. Guests can indulge in a vast spa, enjoy gourmet dining, and relax in elegantly designed rooms with breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains. It serves as an ideal base for both winter sports enthusiasts and summer hikers, promising a serene and invigorating escape in nature's grandeur.
