Monastero San Biagio is a beautifully restored 14th-century monastery nestled in the tranquil Umbrian countryside. This unique venue offers a profound sense of peace and a holistic approach to well-being, making it an ideal destination for retreat groups seeking spiritual calm, rejuvenation, and an authentic Italian experience. Guests can immerse themselves in serene historic surroundings, indulge in tailored wellness programs at the dedicated spa, and enjoy exquisite, locally-sourced Umbrian cuisine, including the monastery's renowned craft beer. It truly represents a harmonious blend of history, nature, and holistic care.
