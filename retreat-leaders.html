Thinking about hosting a retreat in Italy — one day?

Italian Retreat Venues is a curated discovery project connecting retreat leaders with distinctive, authentic venues across Italy.

This page is simply a way to listen and learn.

I’m currently exploring how international yoga and wellness teachers imagine retreats in Italy — from the kind of setting they’re drawn to, to the group size and pace they prefer.

There’s no commitment here. Filling in the form doesn’t mean you’re booking a retreat or joining a mailing list.

It simply helps me build something thoughtful, aligned, and respectful — for both teachers and venues.
