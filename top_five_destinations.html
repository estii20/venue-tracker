Top 5 Yoga-Friendly Venues in Friuli-Venezia Giulia

Discover the hidden gems of Northeast Italy perfect for transformative wellness retreats

Venue Guide 12 min read Published January 2025

Nestled between the Alps and the Adriatic Sea, Friuli-Venezia Giulia offers a unique blend of Italian elegance, Austrian alpine charm, and Slovenian mystique. This northeastern region of Italy has become a hidden sanctuary for wellness retreats, offering everything from coastal luxury to mountain serenity.

As someone who has personally visited and vetted these venues, I'm excited to share the top 5 yoga-friendly locations that consistently deliver exceptional experiences for UK wellness professionals and their retreat participants.
