Frequently Asked Questions

Do I pay any fees for your venue sourcing service?

No, our service is completely free for retreat leaders. We earn our commission directly from the venues we partner with, which comes from their marketing and sales budgets.

Are the rates higher because of your commission?

Actually, no. Our established relationships often allow us to secure better rates than what venues offer for direct bookings. The commission is built into their standard pricing structure.

When is the commission paid?

Commission is typically processed within 30 days of your retreat deposit being confirmed with the venue. This ensures the booking is secure before any payments are made.

What if I need to cancel my retreat?

Cancellation policies follow the venue's standard terms. If a cancellation occurs before commission is paid, no commission is processed. We help mediate any disputes that may arise.

Can I book directly with the venue after you introduce us?

For your initial retreat, we ask that bookings go through our established partnership to honor our agreements. For future retreats, venues may offer direct booking options at their discretion.
